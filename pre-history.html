Vortexledger History & Evolution

After a trip to Switzerland I discovered Bitcoin. I suspected that something new would replace the attacked offshore solutions. As a family man living in Austria, I invested 3,000 EUR in Bitcoin and mining hardware to thoroughly understand the technology.

  • I got to know Namecoin and Litecoin.
  • Greencoin: I started criticizing the waste of PoW (Proof of Work). Bitcointalk thread here.

I participated in the first 100% Greencoin (PoS) projects:

  • NXT: The birth of a great community, from which a thousand small coins branched off.
  • NEM (New Economy Movement): I later rotated my significant stake into IOTA.
  • Horizon (NXT clone): I ran my own node in the Estonian state's blockchain-based notary experiment (5% stake).

I was one of the founding members of the IOTA Foundation (IF) (as ifinta) alongside partners such as Microsoft, Deutsche Bank, Porsche, VW and Bosch.

This project was meant to become the payment instrument for robots and drones (DAG technology). Although I later left, I keep a unique technical backup of the early years. This is where my vision of the machine-to-machine economy crystallized.

  • Curtly: The idea of a "short" blockchain — don't store the whole thing, only the last year.
  • Byteball (OByte): Here I issued EMERALD, one of the first RWA (Real World Asset) backed tokens, and CHARM for online auction houses.

Vortexledger (the first post is still up on LinkedIn — yet another critique of Bitcoin). This is MONEY :) so it stays at the level of a general equivalent, while I argue that barter trade was better. I wrote 50,000 years — a bit of an exaggeration, but that's what came to mind, and we don't really know when humanity began or when money-based payment started. The principle is what matters. With today's technology and as many tickers as exist, reforming barter trade may be feasible. ZSOZSO is a step in that direction — my second attempt at a distributed central bank (CYBERFORINT distributed minting/burning), One use payment system (the development), Multi use payment system (for the support and more).

This is where I realized the payment system of life on Earth and possibly on Saturn's moon Titan. Water as a medium of exchange. An atmosphere of Oxygen (aerobic life, with anaerobic bacteria as a footnote) or Methane (anaerobic life? civilization?).

  • I delved into the significance of 3+D in the context of outer space. I forked OpenSCAD: OpenSC4D. An explanation for Saturn's ring system could be the intersection plane of two 3D spaces. Two worlds meeting? The gateway? Interesting possibilities that explain a few "little things" about our lives.
  • I realized that in 4D our Earth might look different — we simply have no information about a lot of things yet. Gravity. Dark matter… What happens to a photon when it stops? Or does it run forever, making it a perpetual motion particle? A flood of questions arose in me.
  • Speaking of supernovae, black holes… What if during a supernova the star's 3D space "punctures" in a new direction, and a new 3D space is born there (with a big bang)? Could multiple universes exist?
  • I fantasized about what would happen if we pushed a 4D rod (rivet) through into another 3D space. What would they see there? A sphere growing larger and larger out of nothing… The many pebbles around Saturn?
  • I noted on X that life might be found in the upper atmosphere of Venus — and a probe flew there and measured it. Days later I got the measurement results in a NASA article and other papers. There are signs of life.
  • I posted that life in other star systems could be searched for based on the Kuiper Belt, since somewhere between there and the star there must be a planet where water flows — and shortly after, an international research effort was launched. Some journalists tried to reach me on LinkedIn, Bill Gates briefly followed me there — to many people's surprise.
  • "2019/2020 Happy New Year for anaerob bacterias!" — My post on multiple social media platforms, which many people understood. Bill Gates pulled out (?) his book on "decarbonization". Great. They'll solve global warming. The Covid era arrived.
  • Just recently I learned that NASA is planning a robot landing on Titan :D Good luck. Let's hope they don't end up like Icarus :D and we like the builders of the Tower of Babel :D

I found the Stellar network to be the most suitable for realizing my Distributed Central Bank vision. Currently I develop Rust-based Soroban smart contracts and PWA apps (Dioxus, GUNdb).

ZSOZSO (Zs): Utility token for development.
CYBERFORINT (CYF): Test of a new type of distributed currency technology.


Vortexledger történet és fejlődés

Egy svájci út után találtam rá a Bitcoinra. Sejtettem, hogy a támadott offshore megoldásokat valami új váltja fel. Ausztriában élő családfőként 3000 EUR-t fektettem Bitcoinba és bányászgépekbe, hogy alaposan megismerjem a technikát.

  • Megismertem a Namecoin-t és a Litecoin-t.
  • Greencoin: Elkezdtem kritizálni a PoW (Proof of Work) pazarlását. Bitcointalk thread itt.

Részt vettem az első 100% Greencoin (PoS) projektekben:

  • NXT: Egy nagyszerű közösség születése, amiből ezer kis coin ágazott el.
  • NEM (New Economy Movement): Meghatározó stake-emet később IOTA-ba forgattam.
  • Horizon (NXT klón): Saját node-ot futtattam az észt állam blockchain-alapú jegyzői kísérletében (5% stake).

Az IOTA Foundation (IF) egyik alapító tagja voltam (ifinta néven) olyan partnerek mellett, mint a Microsoft, Deutsche Bank, Porsche, VW és a Bosch.

Ez a projekt a robotok és drónok fizetési eszköze lett volna (DAG technológia). Bár később kiléptem belőle, az indulási évekről egyedi technikai backup-ot őrzök. Itt kristályosodott ki a jövőképem a gép-gép közötti gazdaságról.

  • Curtly: A "kurta" blokklánc ötlete – ne tároljuk az egészet, csak az utolsó egy évet.
  • Byteball (OByte): Itt bocsátottam ki az EMERALD-ot, az egyik első RWA (Real World Asset) fedezetű tokent, és a CHARM-ot az online aukciósházak számára.

Vortexledger (LinkedIn-en fenn az első poszt – egy újabb kritikája a Bitcoinnak). Ez PÉNZ :) tehát marad az általános egyenértékes szintjén, míg azt állítom, hogy a cserekereskedelem jobb volt. 50.000 évet írtam, az picit túlzás, de ez jött akkor az eszembe – úgysem tudjuk, mikortól van emberiség és mikortól van pénzfizetés. Az elv a lényeges. A mai technikával és ennyi tickerrel, mint ami létezik, megoldható lehet a cserekereskedelem reformja. A ZSOZSO egy lépés efelé – az elosztott jegybankra tett második kísérletem (CYBERFORINT elosztott kibocsátása/égetése), One use payment system (the development), Multi use payment system (for the support and more).
