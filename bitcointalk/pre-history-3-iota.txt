[b]⭐ Vortexledger Pre-History [Milestone]: IOTA Founding Era — The Foundations of M2M[/b]

I was one of the founding members of the IOTA Foundation (IF) (as ifinta) alongside partners such as [b]Microsoft, Deutsche Bank, Porsche, VW and Bosch[/b].

This project was meant to become the payment instrument for robots and drones (DAG technology). Although I later left, I keep a unique technical backup of the early years. This is where my vision of the machine-to-machine economy crystallized.
